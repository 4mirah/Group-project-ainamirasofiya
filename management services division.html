MANAGEMENT SERVICES DIVISION

Management Service Division is a division that is responsible to equip the services that have in Kelantan Public Library Corporation which is to help the operation and management activity to keep running smoothly.

The Management Service Division has been divided into Director Office, Administration and Human Resource Unit and Finance Unit.
